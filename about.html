Our Mission

PTmeasures is dedicated to revolutionizing physical therapy outcome measure tracking. We aim to empower clinics and practice owners with sophisticated tools to track clinic performance, assess individual clinician effectiveness, and compare data against normative benchmarks. Our mission is to elevate the practice of physical therapy with insightful data analytics.
